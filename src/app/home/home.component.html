What is Spotify?

Spotify transformed music listening forever when it launched in 2008. Discover, manage and share over 100 million tracks and 5 million podcasts titles, for free,
  or upgrade to Spotify Premium to access exclusive features for music including improved sound quality and an on-demand, offline, and ad-free music listening
  experience. Today, Spotify is the world's most popular audio streaming subscription service with 489 million users, including 205 million subscribers in more than
  180 markets.

How to use?

1. Run the app.
  2. Click on "Search Artist Name" at the top bar.
  3. Click on the text box and start searching.
  4. Click the Submit button.
